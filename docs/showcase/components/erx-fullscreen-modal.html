Full-screen modal dialog for immersive experiences like image galleries, video players, or detailed views. Supports multiple animation types.

Basic Fullscreen Modal

Welcome to the Fullscreen Experience

This modal takes over the entire screen, providing an immersive experience for your content. Press Escape or click the X button to close.

Fullscreen modals are perfect for:

  • Image galleries and lightboxes
  • Video players
  • Complex forms that need focus
  • Document viewers
  • Detailed item views
